📱 First time on GoodMarket? Pick the smoothest path for your wallet.

For mobile wallets, opening GoodMarket inside your wallet's built-in browser avoids most connection errors. If you already use GoodWallet for G$ claims, stay on this page and use WalletConnect.

  1. Open your MetaMask mobile app.
  2. Tap Explore on the bottom bar.
  3. Type or paste https://goodmarket.live in the search bar.
  4. Tap Get Started on this page and choose Injected Wallet.

Loading GoodMarket inside MetaMask's in-app browser gives the most reliable claim & save experience.

  1. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app.
  2. Tap Discover on the bottom bar.
  3. Type or paste https://goodmarket.live in the search bar.
  4. Tap Get Started on this page and choose Injected Wallet.

Trust Wallet's Discover browser handles Celo transactions natively — fewer prompts, no chain-switch errors.

  1. Stay on this page in your normal mobile/desktop browser.
  2. Tap Get Started at the top.
  3. Choose Wallet Connect → WalletConnect.
  4. Either scan the QR code with GoodWallet, or tap Copy URI and paste it inside GoodWallet.

Already claiming G$ in GoodWallet? Keep using it — WalletConnect lets you sign GoodMarket transactions without switching wallets.
