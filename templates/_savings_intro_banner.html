🏦 New to Savings? Lock your G$, CELO, or cUSD and earn bonuses — fully on-chain.

GoodMarket Savings lets you set aside tokens for a fixed lock period and earn extra G$ rewards. Your funds stay in your wallet — only you can withdraw.

Savings is a simple way to lock your tokens for a chosen period (e.g. 30, 90, or 180 days) and earn a bonus reward when the lock ends.

  • Pick a token: G$, CELO, or cUSD.
  • Pick a lock duration — longer locks earn higher bonuses.
  • Top-ups merge into the same slot and inherit the original unlock date.
  • When the lock ends, withdraw your principal back to your wallet anytime.

Think of it like a digital piggy bank that pays you back a little extra for waiting.

  • Non-custodial. Tokens are locked in the GoodMarket Savings smart contract on Celo — GoodMarket cannot move, freeze, or take them.
  • Only you can withdraw. Withdrawals are signed by your own wallet; no admin override.
  • On-chain transparent. Every deposit and withdrawal is a public Celo transaction you can verify on CeloScan from any device.
  • No surprise unlocks. Lock period is enforced by the contract — once set, only time can unlock it.

Not financial advice. Smart contracts always carry residual risk; only save what you are comfortable locking for the chosen period.

  • Earn extra G$. Lock-period bonuses pay out on top of your principal.
  • Predictable rewards. The bonus rate is set when you deposit — no surprises.
  • Build a habit. Locking helps you avoid spending impulses and grow your G$ stack over time.
  • Top-up anytime. Add more to the same slot whenever you have spare tokens — same unlock date applies.

Tip: even a small lock of 100 G$ for 30 days lets you try Savings risk-free before committing larger amounts.
