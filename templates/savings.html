What is GoodMarket Savings?
  • 1
    Lock G$, CELO, cUSD, or USDT for any number of days you choose between 1 and 360. Your tokens go directly into a smart contract on Celo Mainnet — no preset durations, just type in what works for you.
  • 2
    Each (token · lockDays) is a slot. If you top up the same token + duration later, the new amount is added to the existing slot — the unlock date does not change. Different durations or different tokens open separate slots.
  • 3
    Funds are frozen until the unlock date — no one (not even GoodMarket) can touch them. Early withdrawal is not allowed; only the depositor can withdraw, and only after maturity.
  • 4
    After the lock ends, click "Withdraw". Your full slot amount (plus any G$ bonus) is returned to your wallet.
  • ★
    Tester bonus (1–29 days): Lock any amount ≥ the per-token minimum (G$ ≥ 1,000 / CELO ≥ 1 / cUSD ≥ 1 / USDT ≥ 1) and earn a flat +30 G$ on withdrawal — paid from the reward pool if funded.
  • ★
    Mid-tier bonus (30–360 days, linear): Deposit ≥ 100,000 G$ / 100 CELO / 100 cUSD / 100 USDT and earn ⌊lockDays × 500 / 30⌋ G$ on withdrawal — roughly 16.67 G$ per day, scaling smoothly with your lock length. Examples: 30d → 500 G$, 60d → 1,000 G$, 90d → 1,500 G$, 180d → 3,000 G$, 360d → 6,000 G$.
  • ★
    Long-loyalty bonus (300–360 days): Lock for at least 300 days with a deposit ≥ 1,000,000 G$ / 1,000 CELO / 1,000 cUSD / 1,000 USDT and earn a flat +20,000 G$ on withdrawal — this replaces the mid-tier value at that size.

Smart Contract Security
  • Only the depositing wallet can withdraw its slots — not the platform, not anyone else.
  • The contract has no owner, no admin, no pause, no emergency withdrawal. Reward pool funds can never be withdrawn by anyone — only used to pay saver bonuses.
  • Early withdrawal is not allowed — once locked, your tokens stay locked until the unlock date you committed to.
  • All transactions are publicly visible on CeloScan.
