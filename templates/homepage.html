A multiplier game where a rocket climbs higher and higher. Cash out before it crashes to lock in your multiplied winnings. Wait too long and lose it all. Timing and nerve are key!

  • 🟣 Words get harder as you progress

Price Prediction

Predict whether crypto prices (BTC, ETH, and more) will go UP ⬆️ or DOWN ⬇️ over a chosen timeframe — 5 minutes, 15 minutes, or 1 hour. Get it right and win virtual tokens.

🌟 Community Stories

Share your GoodDollar journey with the world and earn big monthly rewards for your story.

📖
What is a Community Story?

A Community Story is your personal experience with GoodDollar — how it has helped you, what you've learned, or how you use it in your daily life. Share it on Twitter/X for the world to see.

🗓️
When to Submit

Community Stories opens once a month, from the 26th to the 30th at 12:00 AM UTC. Make sure to submit your story during this window to be eligible for rewards.

📅 Mark your calendar — the window is only 4 days!

🏅
Reward Tiers
  • 🟣 2,000 G$ — Text post with your story
  • 🟣 5,000 G$ — Video post (30 seconds or longer)
  • 🟣 Screenshots and selfies with the app qualify

✅ Rewards are sent after admin review and approval.

📸
How to Submit

Post your GoodDollar story on Twitter/X, then come to GoodMarket during the open window, paste your post link or upload a screenshot, and our team will review and approve it for your G$ reward.

🔗 Referral Program

Invite friends to GoodMarket and both of you earn G$ rewards when they join and get verified.

📨
How to Refer

Every GoodMarket user gets a unique referral link. Share it with friends, family, or on social media. When someone signs up using your link, you're automatically linked as their referrer.

💰
Referral Rewards
  • 🟢 1,000 G$ — You (the referrer) earn
  • 🟢 500 G$ — Your friend (referee) earns
  • 🟢 No limit on how many friends you can refer

🎯 Rewards are disbursed after the referee completes UBI verification.

✅
Requirements

Your referred friend must connect their Web3 wallet and complete UBI verification (a recent GoodDollar claim on the Celo network). Once verified, both rewards are sent automatically.

📊
Track Your Referrals

Log into GoodMarket and check your Referral Dashboard to see how many friends you've referred, how many have verified, and your total referral earnings so far.

📊 UBI Tracker

Analyze any GoodDollar wallet's UBI history, claim patterns, and Ecosystem Coverage — all in one tool.

🔍
What is UBI Tracker?

The UBI Tracker is a powerful analytics tool that lets you look up any GoodDollar wallet address and see its complete Universal Basic Income (UBI) claim history on the Celo blockchain.

📈
What You Can See
  • 🟡 Total G$ claimed over time
  • 🟡 Claim frequency and patterns
  • 🟡 First and last claim dates
  • 🟡 Ecosystem Coverage score
  • 🟡 On-chain transaction history
🌍
Ecosystem Coverage

Ecosystem Coverage measures how actively a wallet engages with the GoodDollar ecosystem — including UBI claims, marketplace activity, and community participation. A higher score shows deeper involvement.

🛡️
Why It Matters

UBI verification is required for most GoodMarket features. The UBI Tracker helps you confirm your eligibility, understand your claim history, and see how you compare in the GoodDollar community.

🔓 Available to all users — no login required to look up a wallet.
